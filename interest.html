Interest 01 / 03

Singing &
Melody

Music has always been my first language. Before I could code, I could hum. Singing is how I process emotion, connect with people, and find silence inside noise.

From Nepali folk melodies to Bollywood ballads, I find something to love in every genre. I've performed at school events and love singing just as much in the shower.

"Music gives a soul to the universe, wings to the mind, flight to the imagination."

— Plato

🎙️ Genres I Love

Nepali folk, Bollywood classics, emotional ballads, and soulful R&B.

🎤 Performances

School events, informal gatherings, and any moment someone asks me to sing.

🎼 Learning Style

Self-taught through listening deeply, humming, and experimenting with pitch and rhythm.

🌟 Favourite Artists

Arijit Singh, Narayan Gopal, and any artist who pours raw emotion into their voice.

Interest 02 / 03

Mimicry &
Performance

Mimicry is the art of deep observation. Every voice has a fingerprint — a rhythm, a pace, an energy. I study these and bring them back to life.

It started as a way to make friends laugh. Now it's sharpened my attention to detail, built my confidence on stage, and taught me that the best performances come from understanding, not just imitation.

"Imitation is the sincerest form of flattery that mediocrity can pay to greatness."

— Oscar Wilde

🎬 What I Mimic

Bollywood actors, animated characters, teachers (playfully!), and regional accents.

🧠 How I Practice

Close listening, slowing down speech patterns, then rebuilding from the inside out.

🎪 Why I Do It

Because making a room laugh is one of the best feelings in the world.

💡 What It Taught Me

Patience, observation, and that every person has a story worth studying.
